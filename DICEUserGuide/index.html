User Guide

No connection, no problem

This app lets you take web content with you when you will be without an internet connection. Each offline website in DICE is called a report.

If you put your iPad into airplane mode, you will see that you can still zoom in and out on this map, because all of the map’s data is stored on your iPad.

Saving your notes, and loading more content

You can write notes about each report by tapping the notes button in the top right hand corner. To save your notes to your computer, plug in your iPad, open iTunes, select your device, click the Apps tab, scroll to the bottom, under the File Sharing section select DICE, click on the notes folder and finally click Save to.

Loading new reports is similar, simply click add, or drag and drop a file into the DICE Documents pane in iTunes.
